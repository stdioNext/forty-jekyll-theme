Data Collection and Privacy

No Personal Data Collection

Meditation Village is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All your meditation statistics, timer settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers or any third parties.

What Stays on Your Device

  • Meditation session history and statistics
  • Timer preferences and settings
  • App preferences and customizations

This data remains entirely on your device and under your control. If you delete the app, this data will be removed from your device.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or advertising networks. Your meditation practice is completely private.
