Meditation Village

Grow your meditation practice, grow your tree

About the App

Meditation Village is a mindful meditation app that helps you build a consistent meditation practice through gamification and community support.

Features

  • Guided and silent meditation sessions
  • Tree growth system that reflects your practice
  • Track your meditation streaks and progress
  • Join communities and meditate together
  • Send loving-kindness (metta) to fellow meditators
  • Multiple meditation tracks including Anapana and Vipassana
